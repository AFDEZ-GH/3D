PRESENTATION
The Survival Simulator allows students to simulate and experiment with Actuarial parametric survival models using the interactive graphical simulator. The aim of this microsite is to enhance the understanding of several features of these models and the variability of their estimations.
The Seasonal Concentration Lab has three areas, excluding this presentation: tutorial, activities and info.
TUTORIAL
In this area the main technical concepts related to Actuarial survival models and associatied functions like annual mortality rates and life expectancies are described.
ACTIVITIES
Six different activities are proposed to simulate and experiment with the graphical simulator. Some of them are self-contained and others require the student to find out some data on statistical sources on the Internet.
INFO
Links to on-line resources about theory and data related to Actuarial survival models.
